AFQT PERCENTILE CALCULATOR





Percentile:

    Please Note:

  • This program automates the universally used process for calculating one's percentile for the AFQT
  • Input the number of correctly answered questions in each section of the AFQT before hitting "Calculate"
      • Arithmetic Reasoning is out of 30
      Mathematics Knowledge is out of 25
      Paragraph Comprehension is out of 15
      Word Knowledge is out of 35
  • 96 is the highest possible score in this calculation because on the real AFQT/ASVAB, extra points are awarded for more difficult test items and there is no distinction when using this calculator.
  • This calculator can be used with any test that has the appropriate amount of test items for each section.
  • If a practice test does not have the correct amount of test items, you may use a percentage to scale it to the necessary size.
